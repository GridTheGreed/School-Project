Curriculum

The Computer Engineering curriculum provides a comprehensive foundation in digital logic, computer organization, and programming, evolving into specialized areas like embedded systems and networking. Emphasizing hands-on experience and adapting to industry trends, which equips students with the skills needed for diverse roles, from hardware design to software development and system administration. Graduates emerge well-prepared for the dynamic landscape of computer engineering.

Graduates are prepared for roles as network administrators, system analysts, and IT professionals in diverse industries.

History

In 2008, ICpEP.se started with 11 schools, including Adamson University, De La Salle University, and Mapua Institute of Technology. Now, it has grown to encompass more than 100 schools nationwide. ICpEP.se encourages collaboration among future professionals, aiming to contribute to national development. With the backing of both industry and academia, our organization aspires to stand out as a leading institution for computer engineers, involving every Filipino computer engineering student in the shared objective of becoming world-class professionals.
