History

In 2008, ICpEP.se started with 11 schools, including Adamson University, De La Salle University, and Mapua Institute of Technology. Now, it has grown to encompass more than 100 schools nationwide. ICpEP.se encourages collaboration among future professionals, aiming to contribute to national development. With the backing of both industry and academia, our organization aspires to stand out as a leading institution for computer engineers, involving every Filipino computer engineering student in the shared objective of becoming world-class professionals.

6th Regional Convention

Welcome to the 6th ICpEP Regional Convention! Students from diverse schools converge to compete in logic breadboarding, programming, quiz bowl, pitching, and many more! This exciting event showcases their skills in computer engineering, promoting collaboration, learning, and friendly competition. This convention offers students the chance to engage with one another, exchanging ideas and exploring the vast language of computer engineering. Competitors and Attendees will have the opportunity to witness and admire the diverse talents and passion demonstrated by the next generation of computer engineers.
